About Me

With a Bachelor's degree in Games Programming and Art Direction from the SAE Institute CDMX campus, I have cultivated a deep understanding of both the technical and creative aspects of game development. I further honed my expertise through a Master Class at the same institution, solidifying my knowledge and skills in this dynamic field.

Throughout my career, I have played a crucial role in leading development teams at notable companies such as Global Hitss, Browith, and Hot Potato. In these positions, I have showcased my problem-solving abilities, goal-oriented mindset, and disciplined approach to deliver successful projects. I thrive in leadership roles, utilizing my strong aptitude for motivating and guiding team members to achieve exceptional results.

One of my key strengths lies in my ability to think laterally and visualize innovative solutions. I approach challenges with a creative mindset, constantly seeking unique approaches to overcome obstacles. Moreover, I am meticulous in my attention to detail, ensuring that all tasks are executed to the highest standard.

In addition to my technical expertise, I possess a passion for staying updated on the latest industry trends and emerging technologies. This commitment to continuous learning allows me to adapt to evolving demands in the programming landscape and deliver cutting-edge solutions.
